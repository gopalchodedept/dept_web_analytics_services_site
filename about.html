How we work

Three Habits

01

Data Layer First

We never build tags on a foundation we have not verified. Every engagement starts with the data layer: spec it, build it, QA it, because everything downstream inherits its quality.

02

QA Everything

If it is not validated, it is not done. Staging checks, launch-day smoke tests, post-launch data comparisons. Trust in the numbers is the product we actually deliver.

03

Platform Honest

Google and Adobe are tools, not religions. We recommend the stack that fits the client's reality, and we hold both to the same standard of rigour.
